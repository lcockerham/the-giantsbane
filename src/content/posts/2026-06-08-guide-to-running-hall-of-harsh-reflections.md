## Arrival in the Free City

Allustan has directed the party to seek out Eligos, his scholarly contact in the Free City, to learn more about the worms of Kyuss. This is a natural hook after Blackwall Keep — the characters have seen the worms twice now and know they're dangerous.

Use the party's first day or two in the city to establish normal Free City life before anything feels off. Give them a chance to resupply, explore, and meet Eligos under what appear to be normal circumstances.

## Seeding the Investigation

The investigation works best if the party feels clever, not railroaded. Plant several independent clues pointing to the same conclusion so they can triangulate.

**Suggested clue layer:**
1. **Behavioral tells** — the replaced Eligos uses an odd turn of phrase, sits on the wrong side of his desk, doesn't remember a shared joke Allustan mentioned
2. **Mechanical tells** — a _detect thoughts_ or _zone of truth_ spell creates an opening; prepare how each doppelganger responds to magical scrutiny (they can't lie under _zone of truth_, but they can stay silent or redirect)

**On player agency:** Resist the urge to force the party toward the conspiracy. If they're suspicious early, let them be right and let that feel earned. If they miss it, have the conspiracy escalate on its own timeline.
